Viewing Team Feedback

You can view and collaborate on feedback that belongs to your team in its own, team-specific inbox, which appears automatically if you're part of an organization in the Apple Developer Program, the Apple Developer Enterprise Program, Apple Business Manager, or Apple School Manager. Team feedback is viewable in Feedback Assistant on iOS 14, iPadOS 14, macOS Big Sur, and on the web at feedbackassistant.apple.com.

Contributing To The Team

Sending feedback to Apple as part of a team is easy. On iOS, tap "New Feedback" under the team inbox. On macOS, select the team inbox and click the compose icon. If you've filed a personal report that you'd like to move to the team inbox, you can do so from the action menu.

Working Together

You can view, respond to a request, or send a new message to Apple for any feedback in your team. The assignee of a team feedback can download any attachments, close the report, and will receive alerts if there is a request from Apple. Team feedback is assigned to its original filer by default, but you can change the assignment to any other member of your organization that is using Feedback Assistant.

Protecting Team Information

Feedback in your team inbox belongs to the organization rather than the person who submitted the report. If the assignee leaves the organization, their feedback doesn't follow them and stays with the team. If a report does not need to be in the team inbox, a team administrator can return it to the original filer.


*Developer teams are supported for organizations in the Apple Developer Program or Apple Developer Enterprise Program. Individual membership is not supported.
